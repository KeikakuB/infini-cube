Objective:

Survive as long as possible as White Cube.

Stay within the smallest score zone A for maximum points. 


CONTROLS:

Move: Arrow Keys

Select Campaign: SpaceBar or Enter (Return)

Go back to Menu: BackSpace

Quit: Escape


Tips and Tricks:

Don't like the difficulty of the default campaigns?

Feel free to make your own or tweak the existing ones.

You can do so by looking at how the default campaigns
are made in the campaigns folder.

Send your own levels to your friends and have fun!
